Privacy Policy

Himalayan Haven

At Himalayan Haven, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us through our website.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

We may collect the following types of personal and non-personal information:

  • Full Name

  • Address

b. Automatically Collected Information:

  • IP Address

We use the collected data for purposes such as:

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share your data in the following cases:

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your user experience. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, but some features of the site may not function properly.
