Cancellation Policy

Effective: October 22, 2025

At Himalayan Haven, we understand that travel plans can change. Below is our cancellation policy for all bookings made through our website.

✓ Free Cancellation Window

You may cancel your reservation free of charge up to 24 hours before your scheduled check-in time.

A full refund will be issued to your original payment method within 5–7 business days.

Late Cancellations

If you cancel less than 24 hours before check-in, you will be charged a cancellation fee equal to one night's stay.

⚠ No-Show Policy

If you do not show up on the day of your reservation and do not notify us in advance, the full amount of the reservation will be charged, and no refund will be issued.

Modification Policy

You may modify your booking (change dates or room type) up to 24 hours before check-in, subject to availability and possible rate adjustments.

How to Cancel

To cancel or modify your reservation, please contact us via:

  • Email: info@himalayanhaven.com
  • Phone: +977-9812345678

Or log into your account and manage your booking online.

Group Bookings

Different cancellation policies may apply to group bookings (5 or more rooms). Please contact us directly for details.

⚡ Non-Refundable Rates

Some discounted rates may be marked as non-refundable at the time of booking. These bookings cannot be canceled or refunded, regardless of notice period.

If you have any questions about our cancellation policy, feel free to reach out to us. We're here to help!
